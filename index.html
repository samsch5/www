What is the Yu-Gi-Oh! card game?

The Yu-Gi-Oh! TRADING CARD GAME lets you take part in the action-packed card game you know from the Yu-Gi-Oh! TV and comic book mega-hits yourself.

In this game, two players duel using a variety of monsters and Spell and Trap cards to defeat the opponent's monsters and be the first to bring the opponent's Life Points to 0.

Everything you need to play is in this deck, but there are thousands more cards you could use. That's why collecting additional cards from booster packs will further strengthen your deck and improve your chance of winning!

This is the official rulebook for the Yu-Gi-Oh! TRADING CARD GAME. It is important to learn how to play correctly, so you should read through this rulebook before playing if you are new to the game. Understanding the rules will help you get the most out of your cards, so you should keep the rulebook handy during your first few games.
